Tortuga Kid's Club

As part of the Family Traditions program at St. Regis, The Tortuga Kid's Club incorporates an educationally based St. Regis children's play center and video games for older children while a separate area offers a variety of stimulating activities for younger children. Children are welcome to access the club with a parent or enroll their children as often as they wish, based on program availability. The Tortuga Kids' Club Programs are available for a half day, full day, or hourly according to our guest's needs. Babysitting services are arranged through the St. Regis Concierge.

Tortuga Kid's Club facilities are complementary, no charges apply while children are accompanied by a guardian. Charges apply when children enjoy Tortuga Kid's Club program under Recreation Attendants supervised childcare services. Every child participating in the program must be at least four (4) years old and no older than twelve (12) years old. Every child participating in the program must be fully potty trained and must be able to re-dress themselves without assistance.

Hours of Operation

Everyday 10:00 AM - 5:00 PM

Pricing

  • Two Hours: 90
  • Half Day: 120 (Snack & juice included)
  • Full Day*: 170 (Lunch, snack & juice included)

Pricing is per child. Pricing does not include taxes or 22% service charge. All activities are subject to availability and may change. Prices are listed in USD.

*Full day is a maximum of five (5) hours.
